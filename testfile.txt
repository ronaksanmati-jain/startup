THE TRAVELLER 

AIRPORT CENTRIC APP 



Creating an all-in-one app that allows travelers to navigate unfamiliar airports with real-time 
information is a relatively untapped startup idea. Though various travel applications exist, 
an app that shows amenities, TSA line wait times, ground transportation options, 
and airport maps could be useful to passengers.
